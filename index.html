BB Sharing:

Bonus block sharing allows you to share your bonus block with a friend (even if its full).

SHARING -

To allow your friend or friends to get into your bonus block, copy the number in the "paste / share" button and send it to them.

That number represents your current bonus block that you are in.

SWITCHING -

If you have received your friends bonus block in number form, switching into it is as easy as replacing the number in the "paste / share" button to their number.

Then pick the newly created bonus block selection that has the number you just inputted and the word "NEW". It should always be at the bottom.

Then click submit, thats it.

Any extra spaces and fake class #'s will assign a random class for that day.

Standard switching

Just select a class like normal and submit... Thats it.
